About us
Consultancy.in is an online platform for the advisory and consulting industry. The website presents the latest news and trends in the sector, follows the developments and publications of consulting firms across 60+ industries and functional areas and provides an overview of career opportunities for professionals interested in working in consultancy.

We are part of the Consultancy.org family. Our international network of consulting platforms draws over 4 million visitors per year.

Career opportunities span jobs in consulting, graduate schemes, apprenticeships and internships in the consulting industry, as well as job openings for professionals that want to work as a consultant outside the consulting industry. Consultancy.in in addition presents company profiles of renowned consulting firms, provides an overview of events and offers key stats and sizes of the consulting industry.

Consultancy.in works together with top tier consulting firms active in India. Globally we work with more than 150 leading consulting firms and with a select number of partner companies.

The newsletter of Consultancy.in contains the most important news of the past week, interviews with consultants, jobs and events. Consultancy.in has four social media channels: LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook and Google+.

Research
On a regular basis the research team of Consultancy.in conducts research into trends and developments in the consulting industry, as well as performs research into trending topics at the intersection of management, economics, organisation and society. Consultancy.in's research team works together with consultancy firms and partner companies, commissioned to deliver research reports several times per year.

Consultancy.org family
